Love beyond language

By Neelanjana 04 August 2020

Happy Socks is a Swedish manufacturer and retailer of socks, underwear, and swimwear. The company was founded in 2008 by Mikael Söderlindh and Viktor Tell who respectively took on the roles as CEO and creative director.

Happy Socks, a high-quality sock, combines unique designs and craftsmanship. (Probably) The world’s most comfortable and colorful tool for spreading happiness was born. This concept is now brought to life by Happy Socks’ talented team of creatives.

Today, Happy Socks has reached 90 countries and every continent. With Concept Stores all around the world, from LA to Tokyo, Happy Socks can be found in over 10,000 top fashion apparel boutiques.

viktor tell, the creative director, a long-time graphic designer and illustrator who found his happy place in the fashion world. His motto: “All play, no work” perfectly captures this fun, creative, expressive success story.

To get office going adults to notice Happy socks. To persuade them to buy socks of their choice rather than always sticking to drab old-fashioned office wear.

Why be insecure when you can be a peacock?

Doing something that scares you needs courage. Confidence comes out of courage. Start small. Get people to notice you, notice your dexterity. You need socks. Eye Catching socks. Happy Socks.

Comes in various colors, patterns and sizes. Let the socks do their part and you do yours.

Define the socks, let them carry you.

Ad objective #2

To persuade people who don’t wear socks at home to wear socks by highlighting the benefits of wearing socks. And telling them why Happy Socks should be their first choice.

A hundred problems, one solution. Sleep.

A nap a day keeps the stress away. Taking tiny power naps during stressful days can make you more alert, more efficient, reduce stress, improve cognitive functioning and keeps your mind healthy.

But there are times when can’t really fall asleep. You want to take a nap but the nap doesn’t want to take you. maybe it’s anxiety or maybe it’s the weather. You just can’t seem to find a comfortable position. It’s too hot or too cold.

Or maybe it’s your feet!

A study from 2007 revealed that adults who wore socks to bed fall asleep faster. Warming up the feet and hands makes the blood vessels dilate, a response that is called vasodilation. This releases heat through the skin and helps to lower core body temperature. This, in turn, sends a message to the brain that it is bedtime.

We can help. Happy Socks, a high-quality sock that combines unique designs and craftsmanship. A comfortable and colorful tool for spreading happiness. Nap in style.

Visual: a pair of feet, with happy socks on, peeking out from under a bedsheet. Image with reduced opacity. Text center (center aligned)
